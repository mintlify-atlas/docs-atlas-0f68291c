---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Launch VRCX, log in, and get oriented with the core interface."
---

This guide walks you through your first session with VRCX, from logging in to enabling optional features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch VRCX">
    Open VRCX from your desktop shortcut or Start menu. If you haven't installed it yet, see the [installation guide](/installation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your VRChat account">
    Enter your VRChat username and password on the login screen. VRCX fully supports two-factor authentication (2FA) — if you have 2FA enabled on your account, you'll be prompted to enter your code after your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the sidebar">
    After logging in, the sidebar gives you access to VRCX's core sections:

    | Section | What it does |
    |---|---|
    | **Friends** | View your friends list, check online status and current locations, browse friendship history, and manage favorites. See [Friends & favorites](/features/friends-and-favorites). |
    | **Feed** | A live stream of friend activity — logins, world joins, avatar changes, and more. See [Feed & game log](/features/feed-and-gamelog). |
    | **GameLog** | A detailed log of in-game events for your current session, including video links playing in your world. See [Feed & game log](/features/feed-and-gamelog). |
    | **Search** | Find users, worlds, avatars, and groups. Paste VRChat IDs or URLs for direct access, or use Quick Search for instant fuzzy matching. |
    | **Notifications** | Manage incoming and outgoing invites and friend requests, and view instance details for invites you receive. |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your dashboard">
    The dashboard lets you build a personalized layout using Feed, GameLog, and Instance widgets. You can create multiple dashboards, each with its own event filters and column configuration. See the [dashboard guide](/features/dashboard) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable optional features">
    Once you're comfortable with the basics, consider enabling these popular optional features:

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Discord Rich Presence" icon="discord" href="/features/discord-rich-presence">
        Show your current world, player count, and a public lobby join button in your Discord profile.
      </Card>
      <Card title="VR overlay" icon="vr-cardboard" href="/features/vr-overlay">
        Display a configurable live feed of events and notifications inside your VR headset.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Themes" icon="palette" href="/customization/themes">
        Install a community theme to give VRCX a completely different look.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You don't need VRChat to be running to use most of VRCX's features. Friend tracking, search, notifications, and dashboard customization all work independently.
</Note>
