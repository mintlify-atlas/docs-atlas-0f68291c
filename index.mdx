VRCX is a feature-rich companion app for VRChat that runs alongside the game on your desktop. It gives you powerful tools for managing your social world: track your friends' activity, browse worlds and avatars, monitor your game log in real time, and customize your experience — all without touching the VRChat client.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download VRCX">
    Grab the latest installer from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/vrcx-team/VRCX/releases/latest). Run `VRCX_Setup.exe` on Windows, or follow the [Linux guide](/troubleshooting/linux) for other platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your VRChat account">
    Launch VRCX and sign in using your VRChat credentials. Two-factor authentication is fully supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Your friends list, feed, and game log are ready immediately. Customize your dashboard layout to see what matters most to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your experience">
    Install a community theme, configure social status presets, and enable VR overlay or Discord Rich Presence to get the full VRCX experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  VRCX uses the official VRChat API and does not modify the game in any way. It is fully compliant with VRChat's terms of service.
</Note>
