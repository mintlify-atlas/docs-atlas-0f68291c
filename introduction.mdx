VRCX is an assistant/companion application for VRChat. It gives you tools to manage friends, track activity, monitor game logs, and customize your experience in ways the VRChat client and website alone cannot provide.

<Note>
  VRCX uses the official VRChat API and does not modify the game in any way. It is not a mod or cheat — it is fully compliant with VRChat's terms of service.
</Note>

## Additional capabilities

- **Activity heatmap** — Visualize a user's online patterns with a day-of-week × hour-of-day heatmap.
- **Social status presets** — Save and quickly apply status and status description combinations from the sidebar.
- **Auto-launch** — Automatically start other apps (such as OSC tools or voice changers) when VRChat launches.
- **Auto-restart** — Automatically restart VRChat and rejoin your last instance after a crash.
- **Export and import** — Export your friends list, avatar list, notes, and favorite groups.
- **VRChat server status** — A status bar indicator alerts you to VRChat outages in real time.
- **Avatar and world management** — Upload and manage avatar and world images and details without Unity.
